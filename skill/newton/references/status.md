# `newton status` (removed)

The **`status`** subcommand is not part of the current Newton CLI.

Inspect workflow execution with **`newton workflow resume`**, **`newton workflow checkpoint list`**, **`newton workflow artifact clean`**, HTTP or WebSocket APIs from **`newton serve`**, and on-disk state under **`.newton/tasks/`** in the workspace you passed to `run` or `batch`. Use `newton --help` and `newton workflow --help` for the exact subcommands on your build.
